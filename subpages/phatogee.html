Pathogee

PREDICTING HEALTH FOR WEALTH
Email LinkedIn Github
Pathogee is an app which combines NASA’s environmental data, publicly available data on pathogen transmission upgraded by citizen science to bring you prediction and prevention against diseases.
Simply open the app and you are ready to go - app will, based on your position and surrounding data, display you prevention measures for predictable pathogens in your area. If you have a pet or a plant select which breed they are and the app will display you prevention measures for them as well. Users of the app can improve the predictions by providing their own feedback: if you or your pet gets sick and if preventive measures worked for you. We’ve used different NASA’s data sources on environmental data:
  • https://aqua.nasa.gov/content/hsb
  • https://wonder.cdc.gov/
  • https://aqua.nasa.gov/content/hsb
We’ve also used publicly available data source on pathogens:
  • https://www.viprbrc.org
